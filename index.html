What's in the pan?

Site Locations

The Critical Zone Pedology Laboratory at UKY began its Fragipan Project in 2019. Fragipans are soil features that restrict downward movement of water and plant root penetration resulting in stunted plant roots and decreasing crop yields.

Soil samples were collected from sites demonstrating fragic properties in an effort to better describe the physical, chemical, and mineralogical characteristics of fragipans. Hover over markers to learn more about individual sites!
